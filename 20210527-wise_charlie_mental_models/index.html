Wise Charlie Mental Models (cards)

Thursday May 27, 2021

These 100 cards are kind of cute. They summarize mental models in the style of Charlie Munger; some are distinct Munger-isms. The descriptions aren't terribly good and there are errors. I like the general idea, and I like cards; these are mediocre but not bad.
