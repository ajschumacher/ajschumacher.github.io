Come work at Meta! (Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp/etc.)

Tuesday November 9, 2021

[Update 2025-04-17]

For about three and a half years, I did open referrals. I referred over 75 people. Two or three got jobs. People I referred were sometimes upset when they didn't get interviews, as if they thought a referral would entitle them to one. Meta has also changed incentives for referrals, and many positions don't accept referrals at all any more. In light of all this, my recommendation now is to apply through the Meta careers site. Good luck!

If you sign up, I'll send you an email update at most monthly with new stuff.

This site also has RSS. You can connect with me via Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub, and email.
