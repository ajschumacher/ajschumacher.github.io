The UK's Seven Principles of Public Life

Tuesday December 30, 2025

  • Selflessness
  • Integrity
  • Objectivity
  • Accountability
  • Openness
  • Honesty
  • Leadership

The UK's Committee on Standards in Public Life was created in response to issues of corruption in the government and published its Seven Principles, also known as the Nolan principles after the committee's initial chair.
