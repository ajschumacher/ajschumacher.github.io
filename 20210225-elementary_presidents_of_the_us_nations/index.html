Elementary Presidents of the US Nations

Thursday February 25, 2021

There are 10 two-letter codes that correspond to both US states and ISO 3166 country codes and (lowercasing the second letter) an element from the periodic table with atomic number corresponding to a US president.

See all 324 data rows as a Google Sheet, or sources and processing in the README.
