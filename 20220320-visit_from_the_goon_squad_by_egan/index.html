A Visit from the Goon Squad, by Egan

Sunday March 20, 2022

“Time's a goon, right?” This book is very much as described on Wikipedia. I enjoyed it. It shows things from different people's perspectives and gives you a lot of things to react to. There's largely no singular narrative, the good aren't rewarded, the bad aren't punished. It's most disappointing when hints of arc or closure appear toward the end.
