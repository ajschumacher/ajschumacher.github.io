# The four cardinal Platonic virtues


 * Wisdom
 * Courage
 * Temperance
 * Justice


The four virtues identified by Plato as charactistic of a perfect
state have been appropriated and promulgated widely since his writing
around 375 BCE.


> And being perfect, is therefore wise and valiant and temperate and
> just. (_Republic_ [book 4][])

[book 4]: https://classics.mit.edu/Plato/republic.5.iv.html#:~:text=And%20being%20perfect%2C%20is%20therefore%20wise%20and%20valiant%20and%20temperate%20and%20just.


These are also the Stoic virtues and the Christian Cardinal virtues,
and they turn up all over the place.
