# The Catholic three theological virtues


 * Faith
 * Hope
 * Charity


These appear in the Christian Bible (1 Corinthians 13:13 is the most
popular reference) and were emphasized by Augustine, but were elevated
to "theological virtues" in the 12th and 13th centuries, most
definitively by Aquinas.


> There are three theological virtues: faith, hope, and charity.
> ([Catholic Catechism][])
