The Catholic three theological virtues

Sunday June 7, 2026

  • Faith
  • Hope
  • Charity

These appear in the Christian Bible (1 Corinthians 13:13 is the most popular reference) and were emphasized by Augustine, but were elevated to "theological virtues" in the 12th and 13th centuries, most definitively by Aquinas.

There are three theological virtues: faith, hope, and charity. (Catholic Catechism)

In Catholic Catechism, these virtues appear alongside "The Human Virtues," which include the four cardinal Platonic virtues.
