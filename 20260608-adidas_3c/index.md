# The Adidas 3Cs


 * Creativity
 * Collaboration
 * Confidence


Adidas used these at least around [2018][], [2019][], [2020][]...
They're fun because they seem so style-focused: three alliterative
values for "the brand with the three stripes." They were part of the
"culture" component of the Adidas "Create the new" strategy effort.
