# The three great pillars of Freemasonry


 * Wisdom
 * Strength
 * Beauty


Freemasons adopted these probably around the 1720s. It seems like they
were trying to bridge from architectural to personal values, possibly
referencing what Vitruvius wrote around 30-15 BCE.


> All these [buildings] should possess strength, utility, and beauty.
> (Vitruvius, [De architectura][])

[De architectura]: https://penelope.uchicago.edu/Thayer/E/Roman/Texts/Vitruvius/1*.html#3.2
